we are going to create a n Online Residence Application system which will help Walter Sisulu University Students

Walter Sisulu University is currently experiencing a huge number of applications for residences from new and returning students .
The shortage of residences has created a great demand and unhealthy competition because some students sleep on the queues for days during the registration period.
To mitigate this unhealthy competition,
We decided to improve the process of application for residences and allocation by introducing an online system that will manage residence applications and allocations.
The growing demand of student residences at WSU raises the significant requirement to manage applications for residences through the use of modern technology.
The use of technology in managing applications becomes more effective and thus overcome the manual system weaknesses.
Understanding the importance and limitations of the residence application process,
Online Residence Application system is a collaborative web application that will provide a platform for WSU prospective and returning students to apply for residences online.
This system will also allocate residence to deserving students and allow residence officers to monitor the progress of each student who did apply online
